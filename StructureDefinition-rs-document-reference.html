Resource Profile: RS DocumentReference (Evidence)

Official URL: https://build.fhir.org/ig/UPM-NTHC/PH-RoadSafetyIG/StructureDefinition/rs-document-reference Version: 0.2.0
Draft as of 2025-11-01 Computable Name: RSDocumentReference

Evidence and post-crash documentation linked to the incident (e.g., cause of crash, party at fault, incident management logs, CCTV, shape files).

Usages:

  • Use this Profile: RS Bundle — EMS Submission, RS Bundle — ONEISS Submission and RS Bundle — Post‑Crash Investigation
  • Refer to this Profile: RS Composition — EMS Submission, RS Composition — ONEISS Submission and RS Composition — Post‑Crash Investigation
  • Examples for this Profile: DocumentReference/rs-bundle-example-documentreference and DocumentReference/rs-example-documentreference

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics
